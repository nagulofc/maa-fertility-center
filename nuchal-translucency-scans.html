Nuchal Translucency Scan (NT Scan)

NT is the sonographic appearance of a collection of fluid under the skin behind the baby’s neck, during the first trimester of pregnancy.

Anomaly Scan:

An anomaly is a mid-pregnancy scan, takes a close look at the baby and womb. This will check the placenta and if baby is developing normally.

Growth Scans

First Growth Scan is done during the last trimester of pregnancy and second growth scan is done close to the due date. These scans determine the growth of the baby.

Fetal Echo

Fetal Echocardiogram or Fetal Echo uses sound waves to determine the unborn baby's heart. This painless test shows the structure of the heart and checks its working status.
