FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the life term of the washed sperm?

    Washed sperm, in general, live for 6-12 hours, but, sometimes as long as 24 to 48 hours. It’s not advised to be kept for too long. Thus, the timing for IUI is so important that it is advisable to do insemination within 3 hours.

  • Can the sperm fall out?

    The sperm doesn’t fall out once it is injected into the uterus.

  • Can IUI be done at home?

    The sperm needs to be washed and thus IUI shouldn’t be done at home. Patients should not attempt to do IUI at home as getting semen or air into the uterus can cause physio-pathological complications.
